Who is Elpa?

Ennerdale Lawley Pool Association, lovingly known as ELPA, was established in 2019. We saw the opportunity and the need to unite Pool players within and around our Community, and to grow the sport.

We started off with 6 teams and about 70 players. All from different backgrounds, but all having one thing in common: The Love Of The Game !

We have successfully laid the foundation for an institution that can only grow and take our players to even greater heights in the future.
