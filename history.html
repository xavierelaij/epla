Our History

Ennerdale Lawley Pool Association ran two major competitions for the year 2019. The Team League Event on the one hand, consisting of 6 teams and about 70 players altogether,  and also an Individual Competition of 24 players altogether, on the other hand. In the team event, each team played against all other teams, and in the Individual event, each player played against all other players.
